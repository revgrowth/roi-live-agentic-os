## HUMANIZER NOTES

Deep mode applied (voice-profile loaded). Patterns checked during drafting:

- **Conversational connectors:** "So," "And," "But," "Here's the thing" used throughout
- **Intensifiers:** Specific numbers over vague claims (60hrs, 4hrs, 1000hrs, 27hrs → 4hrs)
- **Zero banned vocabulary:** No leverage, utilize, unlock, empower, game-changer, deep dive, comprehensive, cutting-edge
- **Zero jargon:** No mentions of Claude Code, n8n, MCP, API, agentic (except in "Agentic Academy" brand name). The system is described by what it does, not what it's built on.
- **Fragments for pacing:** "Again." "80 hours a month." "Not a dead forum."
- **Mixed sentence length:** Short punches (3-8 words) alternating with medium explanations
- **Active voice + "you" address** throughout — no passive hedging
- **AI pattern check:** No "in today's world", no "it's important to note", no robotic parallelism, no rule-of-three abuse
