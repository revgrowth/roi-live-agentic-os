## Carousel 1: The 3 Skills Traps (Value)

**Slide 1:**
"3 mistakes killing your Claude Code output"
*(and the 5-min fix for each)*

**Slide 2:**
"Trap 1: The Hoarding Trap"
Claude has a 15,000 character budget for skills.
50-200 installed = noisy menu = wrong picks = inconsistent output.

**Slide 3:**
"Fix: Curate 20-30 skills"
Audit what you've got.
If you can't explain when one fires vs another — delete one.
Less noise = better output.

**Slide 4:**
"Trap 2: The Copy-Paste Trap"
Marketplace skills don't know your brand.
No voice. No audience. No examples.
It's an unbriefed consultant giving textbook answers.

**Slide 5:**
"Fix: 15 minutes of context"
Install locally.
Add brand voice + 3-5 examples as reference files.
Generic skill → YOUR skill.

**Slide 6:**
"Trap 3: The Monolith Trap"
Everything crammed in one SKILL.md.
Claude loads it all. Every. Single. Run.
Can't debug. Can't iterate.

**Slide 7:**
"Fix: Separate process from knowledge"
SKILL.md = what Claude does (process)
references/ = what Claude knows (context)
Loads only what it needs.

**Slide 8:**
"The best skill setup isn't the biggest."
It's the one where every skill is personalised to YOUR business.
Save this. Audit your skills this week.
