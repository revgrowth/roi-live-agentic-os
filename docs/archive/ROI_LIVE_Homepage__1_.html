How is this different from hiring an agency?

+

Agencies hand you a junior account manager and a monthly report. We give you a dedicated Fractional CMO who actually leads your growth strategy, plus a team of specialists who execute across every channel. You get the strategic brain of a CMO and the execution power of a full agency — without the $200K salary or the agency runaround.

What types of businesses do you work with?

+

We work with ecommerce brands, B2B companies, and local service businesses doing $300K–$3M+ that are ready to scale but stuck — usually because they've outgrown DIY marketing but aren't big enough for a full-time CMO. If you're ambitious and ready to invest in real growth infrastructure, we're your team.

How fast will I see results?

+

East Perry was plateaued at $45K/month for years — they hit their first $100K month within 90 days of working with us. But every business is different. Email and paid ads can show returns in weeks. SEO builds over 3–6 months. What we can guarantee: you'll have a clear strategy, full visibility into your data, and a team executing from day one.

What does it cost?

+

Our engagements start with a strategy call to understand your business, goals, and budget. We build custom packages — there's no one-size-fits-all. What we can tell you: our clients on average spend between 10–15% of revenue on marketing including ad spend, but this varies depending on many factors including business maturity, stage, and how fast you want to grow.
