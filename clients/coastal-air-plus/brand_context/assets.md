### Cross-brand observations

1. **The two brand systems do not share a design language.** CCC uses orange/teal/navy with Teko+Rubik+Roboto. CAP uses golden yellow/teal-navy with Poppins+Source Sans Pro. No shared color, no shared font. This is operationally significant: any brand-transition work in a future phase will need an intentional design convergence decision.
2. **CCC logo alt text is the entity-graph leak** flagged in strategy §2.3. The alt attribute embeds "now part of ... a larger Coastal Air Plus" into the page's accessibility and crawl-side content, mixing the CAP entity into CCC's graph. Recommend changing the alt to a CCC-only description (e.g., `"Coastal Carolina Comfort — HVAC services in Summerville and Charleston, SC"`) as a Week 1 technical fix. Coordinate with the strategy §3.3 brand-hold lock.
3. **CCC's teal #097BAA + navy #092A49 palette** is the color system all new content mockups, email templates, and GBP posts should use on this engagement — not the CAP golden-yellow palette.
