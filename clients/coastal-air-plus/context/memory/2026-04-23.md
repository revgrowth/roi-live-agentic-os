## Session 1

### Goal
Lock in the three strategic decisions that gate all SEO/GBP technical work, and set up the next diagnostic step for the April organic decline.

### Decisions
- **Domain consolidation: deferred to fall/winter 2026.** Both coastalcarolinahvac.com and coastalairplus.com run in parallel through peak season (Apr–Sep). No 301s, no canonical merges, no sitemap changes. Revisit Oct–Jan 2027.
- **GBP architecture: single Summerville listing only.** Not three listings. The Summerville GBP (still branded Coastal Carolina Comfort) is the sole local lead engine — calls, directions, leads, revenue. Charleston and Myrtle Beach metros handled via organic/paid, not additional GBPs.
- **Brand transition: logo only.** Website and GBP both still read Coastal Carolina Comfort. No NAP change, no schema rewrite, no GBP rename. Preserves the "carolina heating and air" #8–15 branded rank equity. CAP site exists as a parallel property, not a replacement.

### Open threads
- Diagnose the April organic decline — with domain/GBP/brand all held constant, the April drop narrows to on-page/technical/backlink factors on the CCC site OR GBP-internal churn (category, reviews, photos, verification, suspension)
- Audit the Summerville GBP — it's the single point of failure/leverage for local lead gen
- Clarify what happens to coastalairplus.com during peak — does it rank for anything, does it bleed authority, is it indexed, is there cannibalization risk
